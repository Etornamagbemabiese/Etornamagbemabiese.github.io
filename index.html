What is Zellit?

Zellit is a mobile-first marketplace built exclusively for college students. By requiring .edu email verification and geofencing each campus, Zellit creates a trusted, student-only environment to buy and sell everything from textbooks to dorm furniture. It's Craigslist, reinvented for Gen Z ‚Äî safer, simpler, and made just for your school.
